# Code of Conduct

This project follows a standard Code of Conduct to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Our standards

- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume good intent.
- Provide constructive feedback in PRs and issues.
- No harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Contact the Design System Lead: `@design-lead` (or replace with email/team alias).
- If the issue involves harassment or legal risk, contact HR or Legal per company policy.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in moderation (warnings, blocking, PR/issue removal) depending on severity. The Design System Lead and HR will handle reports confidentially.
